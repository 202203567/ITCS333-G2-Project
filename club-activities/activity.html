Chess Club Tournament

Academic Competition

Date & Time

April 15, 2025 at 6:00 PM

Location

Student Union Building, Room 302

Organizer

Chess Club

Description

Join us for our weekly chess tournament! Players of all skill levels are welcome to participate. The tournament will follow a Swiss-system format with 4 rounds. Each player will have 15 minutes per game with a 5-second increment.

Prizes will be awarded to the top three finishers. Refreshments will be provided. Don't forget to bring your own chess clock if you have one, though we will have some available for those who need them.

Additional Information

  • Registration starts at 5:30 PM
  • Tournament begins promptly at 6:00 PM
  • Expected to finish around 9:00 PM
  • Maximum capacity: 32 participants
  • Current registrations: 18
